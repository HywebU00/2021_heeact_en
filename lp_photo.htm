● Annual Reports
HEEACT’s annual reports summarize the activities and achievements of the organization. The annual reports not only serve the purpose of adhering to the core value of maintaining transparency in evaluation and accreditation, but also help the external stakeholders and the international community understand how professional higher education accreditation agencies operate in Taiwan.
● Special issue
On its 10th anniversary in 2015, HEEACT published a special issue entitled "10th Anniversary Special Issue: A Decade of Leadership on Quality", reviewing the major milestones and accreditation practices in the past decade while setting the vision of “Professionalism, Integrity, and Excellence” (PIE) with the aim to become an independent, fair and professional accreditation body for higher education.
