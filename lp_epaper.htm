Higher Education Evaluation and Development (HEED) is a quality English journal founded by HEEACT and has been jointly published with Asia-Pacific Quality Network (APQN) since 2014, thus becoming APQN’s membership journal.
HEED welcomes quality papers from subjects of:
1. Higher education and development
2. Quality assurance and evaluation in higher education
3. Research development of higher education and its practices
4. Other topics related higher education and development
HEED articles are now published as open access and are published through Emerald Publishing Group .
