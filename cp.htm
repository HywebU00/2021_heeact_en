Prof. Angela Yung-Chi Hou as new INQAAHE Board Member

  • Date: 2018-03-20
  • Source: 高等教育評鑑中心
  • Updated: 2018-03-20
  • Hit: 123456
  • Editor: cindy124

The world-wide association of organizations, International Network for Quality Assurance Agencies in Higher Education (INQAAHE) has finally announced the election results for 2018 President and Board Directors Elections on April 20. With the highest amount of votes, Executive Director Prof. Dr. Angela Yung-Chi Hou was elected as one of the outstanding board members.

標題二

linktestlinktestThis year’s board election calls for 19 quality assurance expert candidates (nominated) from different parts of the world, candidates who partakes in the developing trends of higher education. The seven newly elected board members, along with the newly elected President, Dr. Susanna Karakhanyan, also Past President and current Member of the Accreditation Commission, National Center for Professional Education Quality Assurance (ANQA), Armenia, will together serve the international cooperation for higher education quality assurance.
